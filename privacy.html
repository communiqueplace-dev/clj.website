Privacy Policy

C.L Khanna Jewellers ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Information you share voluntarily — your name, phone number and message — when you contact us via WhatsApp, the appointment form, or email.
  • This website does not require accounts, does not process payments online, and does not store your data on any server of its own.

How we use it

  • Only to respond to your enquiry, confirm appointments, and serve you better in store.
  • We never sell, rent or share your details with third parties for marketing.

WhatsApp & third-party services

Enquiries from this website open WhatsApp, which is governed by WhatsApp's own privacy terms. Embedded maps are provided by Google Maps under Google's terms.

Your choices

You may ask us at any time to delete your contact details from our records by calling or emailing us.

Cookie Policy

This website uses two kinds of cookies, stored in your browser's localStorage:

  • Essential cookies — they remember your cart, wishlist and session. Required for the site to work and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — only activated with your consent, via Google Analytics 4. They collect anonymous data (pages visited, time on site) to help us improve the site. No personal information is sent to Google, and your IP address is anonymised before any processing.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time using the Cookie Settings link in the footer, or by clearing your browser's localStorage.
